University of Arizona IEEE
Yeeet

Located in ECE 105, our branch runs the IEEE open hack lab where students are able to access a wide array of resources including work space, computers, project storage, electrical tools ranging from soldering stations to digital scopes, an assortment of common parts on stock, and a great work environment. Additionally, the room is staffed by the club officers who are able to help out and answer questions. Please do not hesitate to swing by and introduce yourself. All majors welcome.
